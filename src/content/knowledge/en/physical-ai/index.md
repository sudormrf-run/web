# Physical AI

> A comprehensive guide to AI that understands and acts in the physical world

Physical AI refers to AI that perceives, reasons, and acts in physical environments beyond the digital world. Building on the success of LLMs, VLA (Vision-Language-Action) models are gaining attention, but the journey to realize them comes with unique challenges and opportunities.
